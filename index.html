Welcome to European Racing League! We are a PS4 F1 2018 Racing League with races on Friday & Saturday evenings.

Formula 1

Formula 1 is the top tier in ERL. In this tier the best 20 drivers of ERL are competing with each other. Fomrula 1 takes place on Saturdays at 5PM UK time.

Formula 2

Formula 2 is the second best tier in ERL. In this tier the drivers are close but not yet at the top level. Formula 2 takes place on Fridays at 8PM UK time.

Formula 3

Formula 3 is the lowest but not the least tier in ERL. In this tier there is still a lot of action and if you sign up to this league you will begin in this tier. Formula 3 takes place on Fridays at 6PM UK time
